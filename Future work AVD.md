Continuity Note — Future Exploration Beyond AVD
Today we explored solutions for people with correctable visual impairments through lenses, establishing AVD as an open technical standard focused on adaptive visual rules derived from optometric parameters.

This project represents the first step toward a broader vision:
the creation of adaptive systems capable of responding to different human sensory or physical conditions.

In future iterations, we could explore:

adaptive solutions for auditory impairments, based on audiometric profiles

adaptive solutions for language-processing difficulties, using simplified linguistic models

adaptive solutions for motor or physical disabilities, through interface ergonomics and interaction models

multimodal frameworks that unify visual, auditory and linguistic adaptation

AVD is intentionally designed as an open foundation.
Anyone interested in continuing, extending or transforming this work is invited to build upon it, propose new modules, or explore additional accessibility dimensions.
